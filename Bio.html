Bio

UndercoverOz, Melbourne's quintessential rock covers band, has been taking the Victoria music scene by storm since 2015. With a renewed lineup and a commitment to delivering the best in classic rock, this band brings an electrifying energy to every performance.

Boasting an extensive repertoire of over 100 songs, UndercoverOz offers an unparalleled variety of classic rock hits. From the thunderous riffs of AC/DC to the anthemic melodies of Fleetwood Mac and the timeless classics of Bon Jovi, their setlist is a journey through the very essence of rock history.

What sets UndercoverOz apart is their three lead vocalists—Jess, Shaun, and Toby—whose harmonies create a sonic experience that is nothing short of spectacular. Backed by a full production setup, this type of cover band is a rare gem in today's music scene.

As dedicated rock musicians, they not only excel in performance but also take pride in their technical prowess and top-of-the-line equipment. Their self-contained setup, including a trailer for equipment transportation, keeps costs manageable, making them an ideal choice for any venue or event. With their own PA and lighting system, they're ready to rock any stage, anytime, anywhere.

What truly makes UndercoverOz authentic is their commitment to a 100% live show. They don't rely on backing tracks; instead, they harness the raw energy and flexibility that comes with playing live. This approach allows them to adapt to the crowd's vibe, making each performance a unique and unforgettable experience.

With over 60 combined years in the music industry, the members of UndercoverOz bring a wealth of experience to every gig. Whether it's a corporate event, a pub or club performance, or a private function, you're guaranteed a night of rock and roll magic that will leave you wanting more.

UndercoverOz isn't just a cover band; they're torchbearers of classic rock, keeping the spirit of the genre alive and rocking in Melbourne. Book them for your next event and prepare for a musical journey through the ages that will have everyone on their feet, singing along, and rocking out.
